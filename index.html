Our focus

Platform

Enforge is a next generation, digital-twin based production planning & control platform for discrete manufactured products. Enforge modelling approach captures product and process knowledge to unify enterprise functions: from sales to manufacturing.

Product

Design, develop and commision fit-for-purpose manufacturing applications for your specific needs, powered by Enforge’s robust API.

Research

Constantly improving our product modeling and lifecycle management platform. Interested in new application scenarios of our product-first order-driven approach in manufacturing optimization.

Capabilities

Mass personalization

Define rules for mass-personalization, including “hard” parameters such as dimensions. Generate true dynamic BoMs, allow customers to build the product they have dreamed of.

Order-to-task

Compile personalized product specifications to work orders. Optimize interchangeable parts, calculate batches for minimum production time and material use, enable data-driven decision making.

Design generation

Integrate with your CAD/CAM software to automatically generate designs and specification files. Prepare your preparation in minutes, for any batch size.

Applications

Metallic Cabinets

Metallic Cabinets is a vast product family ranging from household to professional specialized equipment. Cabinets are often customized per customer needs, in dimensions, materials and accessories.

Armoured Doors

Buildings have different structural openings and customers have different requirements for safety and aesthetics, making Armoured Doors a heavily personalized product both in terms of structure and accessories.

Industrial Hoses

Industrial Hoses are critical replacement parts in a plethora of applications since they have a limited lifespan affected by environmental conditions, aging, mechanical fatigue etc. It is a knowledge intensive and highly customizable product with millions of configuration options.
